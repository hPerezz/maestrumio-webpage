Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Overview

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Maestrum collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

2. Webcam & Image Data

Maestrum uses your device’s webcam solely for real-time gesture detection.

  • Webcam data is processed locally on your device
  • No images, videos, or biometric data are recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • Webcam data is not sent to our servers or to any third parties

Gesture processing is performed using the Google MediaPipe library, running locally.

3. Internet Usage

Internet connectivity is used exclusively for license validation. No webcam, image, or gesture data is transmitted over the internet.

4. Information We Collect

We may collect limited personal data, including:

  • Email address (for license delivery and customer support)
  • Purchase and transaction identifiers provided by our payment provider

We do not collect:

  • Webcam images or video
  • Audio recordings
  • Biometric identifiers

5. Third-Party Services

Payments are processed by a third-party payment provider acting as Merchant of Record. We do not store or process payment card information.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide licensing and support
  • Comply with legal and accounting obligations

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be published on our website. Your continued use of Maestrum after any changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
