Quick Start

Up and running
in three steps.

  1. 01

    Add Maestrum as MIDI input in your DAW

    Open your DAW's MIDI settings and select Maestrum as a virtual MIDI input. Works with Ableton, FL Studio, Logic, and any MIDI compatible software.

  2. 02

    Map the control you want to change

    Assign any hand gesture to any parameter: filter cutoff, reverb depth, volume, effects. If your DAW can automate it, Maestrum can drive it.

  3. 03

    Start playing!

    Move your hands. Shape your sound. Your webcam does the rest — no cables, no latency, no interruptions to your creative flow.
