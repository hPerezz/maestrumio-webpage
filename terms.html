2. Product Description

Maestrum is a gesture-based creative software tool for macOS only. The Software uses your device’s webcam locally to detect hand gestures using Electron and the Google MediaPipe library.

Important:

  • Webcam data is processed entirely on-device
  • No images, videos, or webcam data are recorded, stored, or transmitted
  • No webcam data is sent to our servers or to third parties

Internet connectivity is required only for license validation and not for webcam or gesture processing.
